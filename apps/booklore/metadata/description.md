BookLore is a powerful, self-hosted web application designed to organize and manage your personal book collection with elegance and ease. Build your dream library with an intuitive interface, robust metadata management, and seamless multi-user support.

### Features

- **Personal Library**: Organize your books in a beautiful and intuitive interface.
- **Metadata Management**: Automatically fetch book details and manage metadata.
- **Multi-user Support**: Share your library with others or have private collections.
- **BookDrop**: Automatic import of books by simply dropping files into a folder.
- **Self-hosted**: Keep control of your data by hosting it yourself.
